About Our School

Vastigruh Vidyalay Karhati (VVK)

Vastigruh Vidyalay Karhati (VVK) is a Co-educational institution located at Karhati, Baramati in Pune district of Maharashtra, India. We are affiliated with Maharashtra State Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education (MSBSHSE). Vastigruh Vidyalay Karhati offers secondary and higher secondary education.

Our Unique Approach: Agricultural Education

The highlight of our school is its agricultural education program. We teach students about farming and its importance and impact on day to day life of villagers as well as its surrounding environment.

Our Legacy

Seven Decades of Excellence

Established in 1952, Vastigruh Vidyalay Karhati has been serving the educational needs of the Karhati community for over 70 years. Our school was founded with the vision of providing quality education while preserving and promoting agricultural traditions.

Our Guiding Principles

Vision & Mission

The foundation upon which we build excellence in education and character development

Our Vision

To establish ourselves as a premier institution that empowers students through quality education in skill development and technical education. We envision creating a learning ecosystem that fosters innovation, critical thinking, and holistic development.

Our vision extends beyond academic excellence to developing responsible citizens who contribute meaningfully to society while maintaining the highest ethical standards.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality education in a safe, respectful, and inclusive environment that builds a foundation for lifelong learning. We are committed to developing excellent infrastructure with well-equipped workshops that are regularly updated.

Through our comprehensive programs, we aim to develop leadership qualities, interpersonal skills, creativity, and strong values in every student, preparing them for success in their chosen fields and in life.
