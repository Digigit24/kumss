About Krushi Udyog Mul Shikshan Sansthan

“Education opens up the mind, expands it, and allows you to improve your life in so many ways.”

KUMSS is an educational society which mainly focuses upon the overall development of students. It established in the year of 1951, Great visionaries founder of the institute late Dr.Shri.A.J.Agarkar plant a seed of an idea. Now, small plant becomes a big tree with great leadership of KUMSS President and Dy.CM of Maharashtra Shri.Ajitdada Pawar from 2004.

1951

Established with a clear mission for rural development.

All Ages

Primary to ITI and skill training under one ecosystem.

Agri + Skill

Agri-based learning and career-ready skill development.

70+ Years Learning, values, and rural upliftment

Our Campus

Education for different ages, built on a strong rural foundation.

KUMSS is a place where students of different ages gain an education, including Primary school, Secondary High school, Junior College and Industrial as well as Skill training (ITI). Institute is going to developed campus with ideal Agri base education and excellent skill training hub. It works for overall development of rural area with need based education and competitive requirement of students.

  • Primary School, Tembha, Karhati
    Strong foundation learning.
  • VVK High School, Karhati
    Secondary schooling with discipline.
  • New English Medium School, Dhakale
    Modern learning approach with English medium.
  • KUMSS Private Industrial Training Institute, Karhati
    Skill training for real careers.

Skill Training & ITI

Confidence comes from skill. Careers come from training.

Skill creates a self confidence in students to live their life. For this purpose KUMSS started ITI College from July 2020. To create opportunities for the development of talent within the rural area and improve the overall scope and space for under developed sectors KUMSS ITI will become great skill training center in future.

“The goal is simple. Train locally. Grow locally. Build futures without migration.”

Started: July 2020 Focus: Rural talent

President’s Message

Words that reflect the purpose behind KUMSS.

“We are happy to gift a ‘Krushi Udyog Mool Shikshan Sanstha’ to the residents of Baramati (To the all baramatikars). There was hardly any campus repute in western area of Baramati. People have to shift their base to main cities for quality education. I am delighted to have KUMSS which will be a great learning destination in the district and nearby areas and the students will not have to migrate to other places in search of quality education.

We strongly believe that every student is a unique person with his/her own set of abilities, needs and aspirations. We at KUMSS are committed to bring out that uniqueness.

Besides academic excellence, the KUMSS is committed to inculcate in all its students strong ethical values of integrity, politeness, kindness and respect for elders. I firmly believe that education should foster confidence, disciplines, clarity in thoughts and decision making ability to set and achieve goals and above all, social responsibility as a lifelong process.

The unique KUMSS pedagogy backed up with meticulously laid state-of-the-art infrastructure brings excellence in learning, training and placement. KUMSS students are groomed to think out of the box and engulf articulation, innovation, team work and leadership.

The challenge lies in utilizing the changing learning patterns with changes in the methods of instructions coupled with technological advancements, to create an educational experience that will influence the all-round development of student and uphold our commitment towards nation building.”

Chairman: Shri. Ajit Anantrao Pawar Commitment: Nation building
