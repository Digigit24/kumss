About KUMSS

“Education opens up the mind Expands it and allows you to Improve your life in so many ways”

KUMSS is an educational society which mainly focuses upon the overall development of students.

It established in the year of 1951, Great visionaries founder of the institute late Dr.Shri.A.J.Agarkar plant a seed of an idea. Now, small plant becomes a big tree with great leadership of KUMSS President and Dy.CM of Maharashtra Shri.Ajitdada Pawar from 2004.

70+

Years of Excellence

Our Vision

  • Empowering Students: To empower students through quality education in skill development and technical education to prepare a master and leader in his field.
  • World-Class Institution: To empower and position the organization as a world-class progressive, cost-effective, and student-friendly institution providing career-oriented skill development training.
  • Global Leadership: To be a leading global organization that provides transformative education to create leaders and innovators, and generates new knowledge for society and industry.
  • Developing Well-rounded Individuals: Our vision is to develop well-rounded, confident, and responsible individuals who aspire to achieve their full potential. We will do this by providing a welcoming, happy, safe, and supportive learning environment in which everyone is equal and all achievements are celebrated.

Our Mission

  • Empowering Learners: To provide our learners with quality learning services that cater to their individual needs and requirements.
  • Improving Social Well-being: To improve the economic and social well-being of youth through industrial & skill development training for sustained income-generating activities throughout their lives.
  • High-Quality Education: Our Mission is to provide high-quality education, a safe, respectful, and inclusive environment that builds a foundation for life-long learning.
  • Practical Learning Focus: Focus on practical aspects of the course material to make learning a meaningful and interesting experience in our intellectually stimulating campus.
  • Moral and Ethical Values: Work to inculcate high moral and ethical values amongst the students to make them responsible citizens and good human beings.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We uphold the highest standards of integrity in all our actions.
  • Transparency: We are transparent in our dealings and communication, ensuring trust and openness.
  • Quality and Excellence: We strive for excellence in every aspect of our work and are committed to providing the highest quality in our education and services.
  • Ethics and Dignity: We foster a culture of respect, ethics, and dignity in all our interactions.
  • Leadership: We believe in inspiring leadership and encourage everyone to lead by example in both personal and professional spheres.
