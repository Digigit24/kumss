About KUMSS

Empowering Future Leaders Since 1951

KUMSS is an educational society which mainly focuses upon the overall development of students.

Established in the year of 1951, Great visionaries founder of the institute late Dr.Shri.A.J.Agarkar planted a seed of an idea. Now, that small plant has become a big tree under the great leadership of KUMSS President and Dy.CM of Maharashtra Shri.Ajitdada Pawar from 2004.
