Gurukul English Medium School

We believe in “Shaping minds, Touching lives, Building Second Homes.” From the first ORCHIDS’ bud blooming in Hyderabad 18 years ago, to now a chain of 41+ international schools, we have impacted more than 30,000 lives.
Setting newer benchmarks with each academic year, we are considered among the top international schools in Bengaluru, Mumbai, Hyderabad, Pune, Kolkata, Chennai, and Gurugram for imparting quality education.
